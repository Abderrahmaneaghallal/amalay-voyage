Privacy Policy – Amalay.ma

At Amalay.ma, protecting your privacy and personal data is essential. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the measures we put in place to guarantee its security.

1. Data Collection & Purpose

We only collect the personal information necessary for processing and securing your bookings and payments. This includes your name, contact details and payment information.

2. Data Sharing

Your information is shared only with:

  • carriers responsible for your trip
  • online payment services and financial institutions in charge of your transaction

3. Security & Compliance

We apply strict security measures to protect your data, in full compliance with Moroccan Law No. 09-08 relating to the protection of personal data.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, rectify or delete your personal data at any time. You can exercise these rights through your Amalay account or by contacting our technical support.

5. Promotional Communication

You can choose to receive promotional offers from our partner carriers. You can unsubscribe at any time by sending a message to our WhatsApp number: 0500000000.

6. Storage Duration

Your data is kept only for the duration necessary to:

  • complete your trip and the associated financial transaction
  • maintain your account (if active)

7. Account Deletion

For reasons of security and prevention of abuse, any request for account deletion requires a processing time of up to 7 days in order to verify and validate the request.

8. Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • save your preferences
  • improve your browsing experience
  • offer customized content
  • analyze site performance
