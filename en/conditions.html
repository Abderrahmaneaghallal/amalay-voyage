The Terms of Service determine the contractual relationship between Amalay (the Data Controller) and any natural or legal person wishing to book a bus trip via the Amalay.ma website (hereinafter "the User"). Any booking made via the site implies:

  • full and irrevocable acceptance of the Terms of Service,
  • acknowledgment by the User of having read them,
  • waiver of their own purchasing conditions if applicable.

Amalay reserves the right to modify the Terms of Service at any time. Modifications automatically apply to any booking confirmed after they are posted online.

Article 2: Transport Offers

The offers proposed on Amalay.ma correspond to the available bus trips and are presented according to the conditions and seat limits displayed. Operators may update their offers; only the price in effect at the time of booking is binding.

Article 3: Price

The prices displayed on Amalay.ma are expressed in Dirhams (DHS), all taxes included (TTC). Any variation in the VAT rate may be reflected in the fares.

Article 4: Booking

The User selects the offer of their choice on the site, checks the summary, and then validates their booking after accepting the Terms of Service. Une fois confirmée :

  • the booking can no longer be modified by the User,
  • validation constitutes proof of transaction.

The User can create an account, accessible via the sidebar. Account closure can be requested via Amalay.ma/contact. In case of difficulty, the User can contact Amalay by:

  • mail: 8 Place des Alaouites, Av Abou Faris El Marini, Rabat, Morocco
  • phone: +212 (0)5 3000 3000

Article 5: Payment & Confirmation

The booking is only final after full payment. Payment methods:

  1. Cash payment at the partner money transfer network The User presents the pre-booking reference. A receipt is issued to them.
  2. Secure online payment via CMI The User is redirected to a secure interface to enter their banking details.

A confirmation email is sent after payment validation. Subject to the right of withdrawal, no cancellation can be requested after confirmation.

Article 6: Boarding & Seating

Some buses do not have numbered seats: seating is therefore free according to the order of arrival.

The seats chosen during booking are preferences, not guarantees. Operators may reorganize seating for operational reasons. Amalay cannot be held responsible for changes made by operators.

Article 7: Right of Withdrawal

In accordance with Law 31-08 on consumer protection, the User can withdraw within a period of 7 clear days. Refund will be made within a maximum of 15 days. Exceptions (non-changeable, non-cancellable, non-refundable):

  • CTM
  • Supratours
  • SAT
  • Ghazala Trans
  • Supplement Buses

For other operators:

  • Cancellation possible up to 3 hours before departure
  • Cancellation fee: 20%, excluding service fees (non-refundable)
  • Price difference non-refundable in case of modification

Article 8: Access to Buses

Access is reserved for Users having:

  • a valid ticket, or
  • a confirmation received by email via Amalay.

In case of overbooking, the operator must offer a free alternative.

Passengers must present valid identity documents accepted by Moroccan authorities. Their absence does not entitle to any refund or modification.
